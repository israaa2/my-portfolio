The Best Books According to Isra

My top 3 books of all-time so far.

The Four Agreements by Don Miguel Ruiz

This is one of my favorite books because, it simplifies life into simple four rules. And they're all work together every single time.

The Forest by Dr. Mustafa Mahmoud

This is an amazing book because, it has a thing from everything. You can't tell if it's a book or a novel, a story or a poem. You just travel with each word, page and chapter. I loved it becuase, it took me back to our land, house and home. It's familier to me even though, I've never known him or his books before.

The Brothers Karamazov by Fyodor Dostoevsky

It's on my list simply because, it took me to Russia, in the cold winter in 18th century. I was there, I wrote with him, met Alyosha and his mentor "The Elder Zosima".
